Preaching

In 2010, I received a clear and direct call from God to dedicate my life fully to His service. From that moment, I made a firm decision to follow His voice and walk in His purpose for my life.

The vision behind Rejoice Laugh & Live Ministry is deeply rooted in my burden for children. Our mission is to equip children holistically—spiritually, emotionally, and socially—while bringing healing to their hearts from wounds and bitter experiences. Our goal is to reach and impact 1 lakh (100,000) children, helping them find joy, purpose, and identity in Christ.

Testimony

I was born into a Christian family. During my school days, I was deeply involved in church activities and had a strong faith in Jesus. I enjoyed the presence of God and was active in spiritual life throughout my childhood.

However, after completing my 10th grade and entering +1 and +2, my life took a different turn. I fell into bad company and slowly drifted away from the Lord. I got involved in worldly pleasures and began living a sinful life. I neglected my relationship with God and disobeyed my parents, thinking I was living freely—but in reality, I was lost.

One painful incident became a turning point in my life. The very friends I trusted the most betrayed me. I felt completely broken and rejected. The guilt of turning away from God and hurting my parents consumed me. In despair, I came to a dark place and even attempted to end my life.

But God, in His mercy, saved me.

In that moment of deep pain, the Lord spoke to me through His Word: “I found you in your mother’s womb, and I have chosen you for a purpose.” That verse shook me. It reminded me that my life was not an accident, and that God had a plan for me, even before I was born.

I fell into deep repentance, realizing how far I had strayed. I asked God for forgiveness and fully surrendered my life to Jesus Christ. That was the moment of true transformation.

In 2010, after much prayer and seeking, God gave me a vision to start a ministry called Rejoice Laugh & Live — with a mission to “Reach the Unreached.” Since then, I have been walking in His calling, dedicating my life to serve others and share the love and salvation of Jesus Christ.

I am a living testimony of God's grace and purpose. No matter how far we may fall, His love can lift us, restore us, and use us for His glory.

All glory to Jesus!
